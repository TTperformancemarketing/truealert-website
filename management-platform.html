Remote Control & Diagnostics

Unrivaled Remote Control & Diagnostics

Take control of every device in your fleet without rolling a truck. Slash support time and eliminate unnecessary site visits.

Remote Fall Detection Tuning

Adjust fall detection sensitivity per user remotely, so you can optimize performance and eliminate false alarms without ever rolling a truck.

Remote Device Actions

Locate, reboot, or power off devices and trigger audible alerts to find them — all remotely — so you can troubleshoot faster and assist users without a site visit.

Remote Configuration

Manage Do Not Disturb settings and update contact lists on the fly, so you can customize any user's experience instantly without ever touching the device.

Device Diagnostics

View last connection, average battery life, data usage, and signal strength history for any device, giving you the visibility to troubleshoot faster and more accurately.

Proactive Monitoring & Alerting

Stop Reacting. Start Preventing.

ResQHub gives you the visibility to catch potential issues before they impact your customers — and the tools to act instantly when they do.

Real-Time Device Status

Monitor battery levels, signal strength, and connection status across your entire subscriber base — giving you the visibility to manage proactively and get ahead of issues before they reach your customers.

Customizable Caregiver Alerts

Define contacts to receive SMS/Email alerts for SOS events, falls, or low battery — complete with map location — so families stay informed and calls to your center go down.

On-Demand Location

Allow authorized users to locate a device instantly via the portal or a caregiver text, making it a valuable add-on for families and a critical tool when emergencies happen.

Complete Event & Device History

Every device event and programming change is permanently logged, giving you a full audit trail for accountability, troubleshooting, and demonstrating service value to your customers.
