SW 200i

Next-Generation 2-in-1 Safety Solution
Smartwatch Lanyard Pendant 4G GPS Emergency Response

The SW 200i combines professional-grade safety features with everyday wearability. Switch between smartwatch and discreet lanyard pendant — both modes deliver full 4G connectivity, real-time GPS, fall detection, and 2-way voice communication.

⌚
Section 01

Versatile 2-in-1 Design

One device. Two completely different wear modes. No compromises on safety features in either configuration.

⌚
Smartwatch Mode

Worn on the wrist like a standard smartwatch. 1.83" IPS display, quick-release straps, full touch interface. Doesn't look like a medical device.

📿
Lanyard Pendant Mode

Clicks into a discreet pendant case for lanyard wear. Preferred by users who don't want a wrist device. Same full functionality — completely concealed.

⚖️
50.60g — Barely There

At just 50.60g and 14.7mm thin, the SW 200i is lightweight enough for all-day wear without discomfort, even for frail or sensitive users.

Section 02

Core Safety Ecosystem

Every critical safety function is built in — no additional hardware required.

🆘
SOS Alarm

One-press SOS triggers an immediate emergency alert to designated contacts and monitoring centres. Fast, reliable, impossible to miss.

🫁
Fall Detection

Automatic fall detection triggers SOS response even when the user cannot press the button. Critical for seniors and high-risk workers.

🎙️
2-Way Voice

Full duplex 2-way voice communication built in. Responders can speak directly to the user through the device in real time.

Section 03

Always Connected

4G-first with automatic fallback. The SW 200i stays connected wherever your users are.

📶 4G FDD-LTE B1/B2/B4/B5/B7/B12/B13/B17 Primary
📡 3G WCDMA 850/1700/1900/2100 Backup
📻 2G GSM 850/900/1800/1900 Fallback
📍 GPS Real-time location tracking Live
🌐

Triple-Network Coverage

Automatic network handoff means the SW 200i maintains connection across carrier boundaries — essential for users who travel or live in mixed-coverage regions.

Section 04 — Performance Core

Health & Wellness Monitoring

Continuous passive health monitoring with clinical-grade sensor accuracy — all captured on the wrist or pendant, all the time.

❤️
Heart Rate

Continuous optical heart rate monitoring with alert thresholds for abnormal readings.

🌡️
Body Temperature

Real-time body temperature monitoring — critical for detecting fever and health deterioration.

🩸
Blood Pressure

Passive blood pressure tracking without the need for a separate cuff or manual initiation.

💨
Pulse Oximetry (SpO2)

Blood oxygen saturation monitoring with low-SpO2 alerts for respiratory conditions.

👣
Step Counter

Daily activity tracking and step counting for wellness monitoring and mobility assessment.

📊
ASR3603S Chipset

High-performance processor with 76MB RAM + 24MB ROM for fast sensor processing and responsive interface.
